Case Studies :

Data Analytics for Supply Chain Optimization

  • Problem : A major retail corporation was facing rising logistics and transportation costs, leading to inefficiencies in their supply chain. Additionally, unpredictable delays were causing issues in stock availability, impacting customer satisfaction.
  • Solution : Through advanced data analytics, they monitored sales, inventory, and transportation data in real-time. Predictive analytics tools were deployed to optimize route planning and anticipate demand fluctuations.
  • Result : The company reduced transportation costs by 10% and delivery times by 5%, while improving stock availability by 15%, ensuring timely restocking and better customer experiences. This approach saved millions annually and strengthened customer loyalty​.
